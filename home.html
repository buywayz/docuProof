How it works

From file to permanent proof in three steps.

1. You choose a file

Your browser computes a SHA-256 fingerprint locally. The file itself is never uploaded or stored.

2. We queue your fingerprint on Bitcoin

docuProof batches many fingerprints into a single Bitcoin transaction, creating a permanent, tamper-evident record.

3. You keep the receipt

You receive a PDF certificate with a Proof ID, Quick Verify ID, and public verify URL. Anyone can check it independently at any time.

Safety & boundaries

What docuProof is — and what it is not.

docuProof is…

  • A way to prove a specific file existed at a specific time.
  • A Bitcoin-anchored, tamper-evident record.
  • A complement to, not a replacement for, legal advice.

docuProof is not…

  • A notary or legal opinion.
  • Cloud storage or backup for your documents.
  • A magic shield against all possible disputes.

You keep custody of your original file. docuProof gives you an independently verifiable timestamp that strengthens your story when you need to show what existed, when.
